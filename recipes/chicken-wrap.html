Chicken wrap

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Garlic sauce
  • Salad mix
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Cut you chicken breast into cubes
  2. Season your meat with salt and pepper, and then fry it on medium heat
  3. Prepare your tortilla wraps and your veggies
  4. Place your veggies onto the wraps, add meat and garlic sauce. Roll the wraps
  5. Bon Apetit!
