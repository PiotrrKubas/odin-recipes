Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomoto pulp
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta using instructions from the label
  2. While your pasta is cooking, cut onion and garlic into cubes
  3. Heat olive oil on the pan
  4. Add onion onto the pan, after 2-3 minutes add garlic
  5. Add ground beef onto the pan
  6. When meat is cooked add tomato pulp
  7. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, basil and oregano
  8. Put your pasta onto the plate, add sauce on top of it
  9. You can use a bit more of basil on the sauce, or add some cheese if you like
  10. Bon Apetit!
